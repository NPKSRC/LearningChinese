# Chinese Lesson Deck — User Guide

**Version 2.0.0**

A pocket study app for the Batch 01 华语 vocabulary, in Chinese with Thai, English, and
(AI-generated) Japanese. It runs in any browser, works offline once loaded, tracks a separate
growth profile for each learner, and saves everything on your own device.

---

## 1. Getting started

### On a phone (recommended)
1. Open the app's web address in your mobile browser (Safari on iPhone, Chrome on Android).
2. Add it to your home screen so it opens full-screen like a real app:
   - **iPhone / Safari:** **Share** → **Add to Home Screen** → **Add**.
   - **Android / Chrome:** **⋮** menu → **Add to Home screen** (or **Install app**).
3. The first time you open it, you'll be asked to **create a profile** — type a name and pick a
   companion (dragon, phoenix, or warrior). Then you're in.

### On a computer
Open the web address, or double-click the HTML file. Everything works the same; you can also use
the **← →** arrow keys to move between cards and **spacebar** to flip.

---

## 2. Profiles

Each learner gets their own profile, so several people can share the app and keep separate
progress and growth.

- Your current profile name shows in the **top-right**. Tap it to jump to the **Me** tab.
- In **Me**, you can **switch** between profiles, or tap **+ New profile** to add one.
- Everything (words seen, difficulty tags, quiz history, XP, streak, chosen mascot) is saved
  **per profile**.

> **Please note:** profiles are *not* password-protected and live only in this browser on this
> device. They don't sync to other phones or computers, and anyone using the device can open any
> profile. This is the trade-off for a free app with no server. (A real account system with
> cross-device sync can be added later if you want it.)

## 3. The four tabs

The bottom bar switches between Study, Quiz, Browse, and Me.

### 📕 Study — swipe-through flashcards
- The Chinese word is always on the **front**. **Tap the card to flip** and see the meanings.
- **Swipe left or right** to move freely between cards — backward and forward, as much as you
  like. You can also use the **‹ ›arrows** above the card, or the keyboard arrows on a computer.
- The **"Show" row** above the card has checkboxes for **Pinyin / ไทย / EN / 日本語**. Tick whichever
  you want to appear on the back; your choice is remembered. The 日本語 line is marked **AI**
  because it's machine-generated (see §6).
- **Difficulty tags (optional):** below the card, **Hard / Medium / Easy** let you mark a word.
  This is purely a label to help you and your stats — it does **not** move the card and isn't
  required. Tap the active tag again to clear it. A colored bar on the card shows the current tag.
- **Lesson chips** at the top let you study **All** lessons or just **Lesson 1–6**.
- The **🔊** button speaks the Chinese aloud (needs a Chinese voice on your device).

### 📝 Quiz — test yourself
- A 10-question mix of **multiple choice** (drawn from the vocabulary) and the original
  **fill-in-the-blank** questions from your worksheets.
- A **中→ไทย / ไทย→中** toggle on the start screen sets the recall direction.
- **Skip — do this one later** moves a question to the end of the quiz instead of answering it
  now; skipping never counts against your accuracy.
- Correct answers turn green, wrong ones red, and the right choice is highlighted so you learn
  from mistakes.

### 📋 Browse — look up any word
- A searchable list of all words; the search box matches 汉字, Pinyin, Thai, or English.
- **Tap a row** to hear the word spoken.
- Each row shows a **colored dot** for your difficulty tag and an **L1–L6** lesson marker.

### 📊 Me — your growth
This is where your learning becomes visible. See §4.

## 4. Growth & XP

Your companion grows as you study, to make progress visible and motivating.

- **Five stages:** Egg → Hatchling → Young → Grown → **Legendary**.
- **XP comes from a balanced mix:**
  - Viewing a new word: **+2 XP** (first time only)
  - A quiz answer: **+5** if correct, **+1** if not
  - A **daily-return bonus**: **+10 XP** the first time you open the app each day, which also
    builds your **🔥 streak**
- **Stage thresholds (total XP):** Hatchling 40 · Young 120 · Grown 280 · Legendary 560.
- When you cross a threshold, your companion **evolves** with a celebration banner.
- You can **change your companion** (dragon / phoenix / warrior) anytime in the Me tab — it just
  re-skins your creature and keeps all your XP.
- The Me tab also shows your **stats** (words viewed, quiz accuracy, words tagged Hard/Easy) and a
  bar breaking down your difficulty tags.

To reset a single profile's progress, use **Reset this profile's progress** at the bottom of Me.

## 5. Controls quick reference

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Move to next / previous card | Swipe left / right, tap ‹ ›, or ← → keys |
| Flip a card | Tap it (or spacebar) |
| Choose languages on the back | "Show" checkboxes above the card |
| Tag a word's difficulty | Hard / Medium / Easy under the card (optional) |
| Hear the Chinese | 🔊 on cards and Browse rows |
| Study one lesson | Lesson chips at the top of Study |
| Skip a quiz question | "Skip — do this one later" |
| Switch / add profile | Me tab, or the name in the top-right |

## 6. Good to know

- **Offline:** after the first load, the app runs without internet. Only the web fonts and the
  speech voice benefit from a connection.
- **Privacy:** nothing is uploaded; all profiles and progress stay in your browser's local storage.
- **Japanese is AI-generated.** Thai and English come from your curated source files, but the
  Japanese was produced by AI and is marked with an amber **AI** badge. Treat it as a helpful aid,
  not verified course content.
- **Thai is from your source**, left as provided. (A handful of optional refinements were flagged
  separately for your confirmation but not applied.)
- **Content source:** Batch 01 — 105 vocabulary items (39 with example sentences) and 5
  fill-in-the-blank questions across 6 lessons. The app is built to absorb more lesson files.
